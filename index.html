As a data scientist, I look to solve problems by using logic. I am a conscientious individual who firmly believes in the importance of structure. The rewarding feelings that result from being a part of a successful team drive me to be an effective leader.

I am a recent graduate of the full-time data science course at General Assembly, where I learned Python for data analysis. The course was an immersive program focused on real-world applications of data science principles and best practices. This included data acquisition and wrangling, data cleaning, data visualization, predictive modeling, and machine learning.

I have strong interests in webscraping and data vizualization, but I expect to expand my data science skills to highly valued areas such as neural networks and big data. The largest passion of mine is sports analytics, and I hope to someday find myself working for the front office of a sports team.
