As a data scientist, I strive to solve problems using logic. I am a conscientious individual who firmly believes in the importance of structure. The rewarding feelings that result from being a part of a successful team drive me to be an effective leader.

I currently work as a Senior Consultant at MorganFranklin Consulting, where, as a member of the data science team, I apply data analysis and machine learning to help our clients make better business decisions. Prior to starting at MorganFranklin, I graduated from the full-time data science course at General Assembly, which is how I began my journey into the field of data science. The course was an intense 12-week program focused on real-world applications of data science principles and best practices. This included leveraging Python and SQL for data collection and wrangling, data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization.

I enjoy utilizing my time both on-the-job and outside of work to expand my knowledge and experience of neural networks and big data tools. Most recently, I have been exploring Udemy courses that teach the fundamentals of useful tools like Apache Spark and Hadoop. Other fascinating courses I have completed include the five courses that make up the deeplearning.ai specialization, which is offered on Coursera and is taught by Andrew Ng, one of the great pioneers of AI.
